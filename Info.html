General Information

    

Sspace provides Wellesley College students with safe night locations that they can go to. You can navigate the application through the main nav bar below. The first page is a map, where you can see the rating of places around your current location and click on them to access their pages. The second page is the "You" page, where you can see past comments that you have made. The third page allows you to see all the locations ranked by their safe score/rating and you can search through them. The fourth and last page allows you to find out more about the app.

    
    
        

Locations near me?

    

By clicking on "map" on the nav bar, you can see your current location as a star and the locations around you as map drops. By clicking once on the drop, you can see more information about that particular location and by clicking twice, you will be brought to that locations page to view more information and past/top comments.

    
    
        

Search for a location?

    

By clicking on the "search" on the nav bar, you can see all the locations ranked by their rating and then you can search through them by typing in the search bar.

    
    
        

Best-rated location?

    

By clicking on the "search" on the nav bar, you will see all the locations ranked by their rating (top ones have the best score).
