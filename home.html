Created by Christina Pollais '16, Sasha Levy '15, and Steffi Lee '15

Welcome to the Sspace website!!

The project Sspace is created as a final project for CS220: Human-Computer Interaction. It aims to provide a safe space for Wellesley students to post reviews/comments about Boston's nightlife. To see the different phases of the project, please click on the correct phase on the bar at the top of the page.
